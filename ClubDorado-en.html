Golden Club

Daily Cognitive Activities

Memory workshops, mind games, and activities that stimulate the mind and maintain cognitive acuity. Activities such as puzzles, strategy games, and memory exercises are included.

Adapted Physical Exercise

Exercise classes designed to improve mobility, balance, and flexibility. These programs include guided walks, stretching exercises, all led by instructors specializing in physical activity for older adults.

Medical Supervision During the Day

Constant medical care and supervision while older adults participate in the day program. This includes monitoring vital signs, administering medications, and attending to any medical needs that may arise during the day.

Healthy Meals and Snacks

Balanced lunches and healthy snacks are provided throughout the day, prepared by chefs who work closely with nutritionists to ensure meals are nutritious and appetizing. Social Interaction and Recreational Activities

A vibrant environment where seniors can socialize and participate in recreational activities, such as arts and crafts, art classes, music, movies and discussion groups. This encourages social interaction and helps maintain a sense of community.

Regular Communication with Family Members

Daily reports on the participant's activities and well-being sent to family members. In addition, family members can participate in special events and activities organized by the center, maintaining a close link to their loved one's well-being.

Roundtrip Transportation

Safe and timely transportation services to and from the day center, facilitating smooth daily participation for the senior or their family.
