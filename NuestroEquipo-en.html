Our great team

Who are we?

Edad Dorada is a comprehensive wellness center for older adults, strategically located in El Coyol de Alajuela. We offer permanent and temporary stays, post-operative care, and a high-quality day center, all within elegant, secure facilities surrounded by nature.

Our care model is based on respect, empathy, and professionalism. Each resident is supported by a multidisciplinary team committed to their physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being.


Why trust us?

Because we deeply understand what it means to entrust the care of a loved one. Our founder, drawing from personal experience, accompanied her father through the final stage of his life, and that journey inspired the heart of this project.
