Temporary or permanent stay

Comfortable and safe rooms

Private or shared rooms, fully customizable to the resident's needs and preferences, with options for personalized decor to create a welcoming and family-friendly atmosphere. Each room has a spacious bed (full or queen), a closet, and emergency systems, with spaces adapted to the accessibility standards established by Law 7600.

Personalized Meal Plan

Menus designed by a nutritionist, adapted to the dietary needs and personal preferences of each resident.¬†Three main meals and two snacks are offered daily, with options for special diets such as low sodium, diabetic or high fiber.¬†In addition, resident participation in menu planning is encouraged.

Access to Daily Activities and Special Programs

A full calendar of activities including gardening, adapted yoga, art classes, live music sessions, book clubs and social events.¬†These activities are designed to keep the mind active, the body moving and encourage social interaction.

Constant Medical Supervision

24/7 medical care with health professionals specializing in geriatrics. Regular health monitoring, medication management and access to specialists as needed are included.¬†In addition, regular health assessments are conducted to adjust individual care plans.¬†

Regular Communication and Support for Family Members

Regular, detailed reports on the resident's health status and well-being are sent directly to family members.¬†In addition, regular meetings are arranged to discuss the resident's progress, along with educational workshops for family members on elder care, chronic disease management and long-term planning.

Access to Green Areas and Natural Environment

The center is surrounded by nature, providing a tranquil and rejuvenating environment. Residents have access to gardens, walking trails and outdoor seating areas, promoting connection with nature and relaxation.

Transportation and Additional Facilities

Transportation services for medical appointments and outside activities.

Spa

We offer a spa service where residents have the option of enjoying a relaxing massage, facial treatments, manicures, pedicures, and hair styling.
