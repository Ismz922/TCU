About Golden Age

In the midst of a natural paradise, where peace and beauty intertwine, we have created a loving and caring home for seniors.¬† Our center is not just a place of care; it is a sanctuary where every detail reflects our devotion to the well-being of those who reside here.

Surrounded by lush gardens and the serenity of nature, our residents enjoy an environment that nurtures both body and soul.¬† The rooms are cozy and the outdoor spaces offer a haven of tranquility, while our deeply committed staff provides care that goes beyond the conventional.

Welcome to a place where nature and a passion for caregiving come together to offer a full and dignified life.

Our Mission

To offer seniors a space of integral wellness in contact with nature, promoting their physical, emotional and social health through personalized care, outdoor recreational activities and specialized health services that allow them to maintain their dignity and independence, in a safe, respectful and peaceful environment that favors their quality of life and connection with the natural environment

Our Vision

To be a comprehensive care center for seniors, recognized for excellence in providing personalized loving care in a warm and welcoming environment, under a nationally recognized model.
