Postoperative Care

Constant Medical Supervision

Medical care and intensive monitoring of vital signs, with regular visits from physicians specialized in geriatrics and physiotherapy.¬† Rigorous control of postoperative recovery is guaranteed, ensuring that each patient receives the necessary treatment for optimal recovery.

Personalized Rehabilitation Programs

Physiotherapy and rehabilitation programs designed to restore the patient's mobility, strength and balance.¬† Rehabilitation sessions are tailored to individual needs and are supervised by physiotherapists who are experts in postoperative recovery.

Specialized Recovery Diets

Specific meal plans to accelerate recovery, designed by nutritionists who consider the patient's dietary restrictions and energy needs.¬†Meals are prepared with fresh ingredients and served at appropriate times to maximize nutrient absorption.

Emotional and psychological support

Nursing staff will provide emotional support to help patients manage postoperative stress and anxiety. The staff will work with the patient to develop coping strategies and provide an emotionally supportive environment.

Regular Counseling and Communication for Family Members

Regular reports on the patient's progress are sent to family members to keep them informed and reassured.¬†Counseling is also offered to help family members understand the recovery process and how to support their loved one upon returning home.

Access to Special Activities and Programs During Recovery

While recovering, patients have the opportunity to participate in recreational activities and occupational therapies, such as painting, music and gardening, that are designed to enhance their emotional and physical well-being.

Private Rooms with Access to Nature

Private, comfortable recovery spaces with direct access to gardens and outdoor seating areas, creating a relaxing environment conducive to healing.

Additional Facilities and Transportation

Transportation services for additional medical appointments and outpatient consultations, as well as facilities for family members to visit the patient and participate in the recovery process.

Spa

We offer a spa service where residents have the option of enjoying a relaxing massage, facial treatments, manicures, pedicures, and hair styling.
